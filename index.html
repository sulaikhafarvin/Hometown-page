Top three activities to do at Kerala

Get Outdoors at Eravikulam National Park

Hidden in this gem of nature is the famous Eravikulam National Park, the home and sanctuary of the Nilgiri Tahr.

Cruise the Backwaters on a Houseboat

Houseboats are well equipped with all the modern amenities for comfortable stay of tourist.

Check Out Munnar's Tea Estates and Gardens

Munnar is the largest tea growing region. These tea gardens were developed in the late 19th century.
